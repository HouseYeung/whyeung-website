Product

KaiwaKit is designed to make live multilingual conversations easier to follow.

KaiwaKit is a browser-based translation product concept focused on three simple jobs: capture spoken language, present a clear translation quickly, and suggest concise replies when the user needs help responding.

Product Scope

The product direction is centered on speed, clarity, and usable output.

Speech to text

Capture spoken input and turn it into readable text quickly inside the browser.

Translation output

Present translated content in a clear format that helps the user stay oriented.

Suggested replies

Offer short, natural response suggestions when users need help replying with confidence.

Use Cases

KaiwaKit is intended for real-time moments where understanding and response speed both matter.

01

Calls and appointments

Understand scheduling details, confirmations, and follow-up questions in live conversations.

02

Work interactions

Stay aligned during meetings, updates, and operational conversations with less friction.

03

Everyday communication

Reduce hesitation during support calls, service interactions, travel, and daily coordination.

Experience Principles

The product is being shaped around a few practical principles.

Minimal cognitive load

Surface only the information a user needs right now instead of overwhelming the interface.

Fast visible feedback

Show progress early so users feel oriented while speech recognition and translation are in motion.

Practical language support

Optimize for usable understanding and usable replies rather than abstract translation quality alone.
