Current product direction

Our main product initiative is KaiwaKit, a translation assistant centered on live listening, translated output, and suggested replies for spoken conversations.

Product

KaiwaKit helps users follow a conversation and answer naturally.

The product vision is simple: reduce friction in multilingual communication, especially in high-pressure moments where users need clarity quickly.

Live speech understanding

Capture spoken dialogue in the browser and convert it into readable text as quickly as possible for the user.

Fast translation

Turn incoming speech into clear translated output so the user can keep up with the conversation without switching apps.

Suggested replies

Provide short, natural responses that users can speak directly when they need help answering in the target language.

Scenario-aware design

Support common real-world contexts such as restaurant calls, clinics, city halls, customer service calls, and work meetings.

Use Cases

Made for moments where a missed sentence matters.

Restaurant reservations

Follow booking details, understand times and seat availability, and reply politely during a phone call.

Clinic and hospital visits

Catch symptom questions, understand basic instructions, and prepare a clear response.

City hall procedures

Navigate formal language during residency, insurance, and document-related visits.

Work and everyday calls

Stay oriented in meetings, delivery calls, landlord communication, and general daily interactions.
